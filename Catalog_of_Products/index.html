Catalog of materials needed for a paint project!

Below you will find a catalog of items necessary for a paint project.

Paint is a liquid coating applied to surfaces, typically walls, ceilings, or furniture, to add color and protect them. It comes in various types, such as latex or oil-based, and finishes like matte, satin, or gloss, offering both aesthetic enhancement and surface protection.

Primer is a preparatory coating applied before paint to improve adhesion, enhance coverage, and ensure a consistent finish. It's particularly useful when painting over porous or dark surfaces, helping paint adhere better and appear more vibrant.

A paint roller is a cylindrical tool with a handle that's used to apply paint to larger, flat surfaces, such as walls and ceilings. It's efficient for covering expansive areas quickly, providing a more even finish than brushes.

A paintbrush is a hand-held tool with bristles used to apply paint to smaller areas, edges, and details. Brushes come in different shapes and sizes, with various bristle types, allowing for precise control and smooth application of paint.

Plastic sheeting is a protective barrier made of plastic material, often used to cover and shield floors, furniture, or other items from paint splatter and dust during a painting project. It helps maintain a tidy workspace and prevents damage to surfaces not intended for painting.

Painter's tape is a masking tape with a mild adhesive that's designed for temporary attachment. It's used to create clean, straight lines and protect areas from accidental paint splatters or bleed-through when applied to edges, trim, or adjacent surfaces.
